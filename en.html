Services
We model pollutant dispersion in all media and develop multi-criteria analyses to asess environmental risk of a project:

Air

Air pollution is the greatest risk posed by the environment in the Americas. Using advanced models a company can demonstrate compliance.

  • Stacks

    we model all kinds of point sources, either vapours, combustion products or outlets of industrial ventilation systems.

  • Tanks

    We model hidrocarbon storage tanks emissions, constructive parameters and operations are taking into account.

  • Odor

    Several industries have compliance requirements with odor nuisance standards, we model static and dynamic odor dispersion for waste management companies and waste water tratment plants.

  • Particulate matter

    Dust dispersion may be caused by any large civil works or mining project. Different classes are modelled as PM10 and PM2.5 in order to evaluate health impact.

Water

Pollution transport by water is one of the most common concerns regarding industry environmental impact.

  • Surface

    We apply fluid dynamics modelling to predict pollutant transport either in streams or static water bodies.

  • Subsurface

    Complex interactions are modelled to determine transport of pollutants between vadose and saturated zones.

Risk analysis

Environmental risk analysis takes into account many information sources and provides under a sound probabilistic framework enough information to demonstrate compliance and responsable operation procedures.

  • RBCA

    Pollution diacharge events requiere an extensive remediation process to reclaim the initial environmental conditions of the site. A detailed multi tier model could determine pollutant concentration targets that guarantee no risk exposure to nearby receptors.

  • Alternative scenarios

    A comprehensive Environmental Impact Asessment takes into account several alternative scenarios. Modeling tools help taking into account environmental complexity to feed a multiple criteria process for alternative selection.
